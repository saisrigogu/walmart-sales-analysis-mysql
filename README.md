## Walmart Sales Analysis Using MySQL

### Purpose  
This project focuses on analyzing Walmart transactional sales data using MySQL to understand business performance, customer behavior, product trends, and profitability. The analysis provides actionable insights that can support retail decision-making related to sales growth, cost optimization, and operational efficiency.

---

### Project Scope  

**Data Analysis**  
Analyzed Walmart sales transaction data containing invoice details, branch and city information, product categories, customer demographics, pricing, cost, profit metrics, payment methods, purchase time, and customer ratings.

**Sales & Profit Analysis**  
Evaluated overall revenue and profit performance, along with branch-wise and product-wise sales contribution to identify high-performing and underperforming areas.

**Customer Behavior Analysis**  
Analyzed sales patterns based on customer type and gender to understand purchasing behavior and customer contribution to total revenue.

**Time-Based Trend Analysis**  
Performed monthly and hourly sales analysis to identify seasonal patterns and peak business hours for operational planning.

**Payment Method Analysis**  
Studied transaction volume and average transaction value across different payment methods to understand customer payment preferences.

**Profitability & Margin Analysis**  
Analyzed profit contribution by branch and calculated profit margins by product line to assess pricing effectiveness and cost efficiency.

**Customer Satisfaction Analysis**  
Evaluated customer ratings across product lines and branches to identify factors affecting customer experience and satisfaction.

---

### Impact and Benefits  

Improved visibility into sales and profit performance across branches and product categories.  
Identified key revenue- and profit-driving products and locations.  
Highlighted peak sales periods to support better staffing and operational decisions.  
Provided insights into customer behavior and payment preferences for targeted marketing strategies.  
Enabled data-driven evaluation of customer satisfaction and its relationship with sales performance.
